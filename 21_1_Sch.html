Signal Modulation Automation System for Quantum Magnetometer

Caoyi Zou*
East China University of Science and Technology
Sep.2021 - Jun.2022
*Indicates Equal Contribution

Schlieren of synchronized candle flame oscillations

Abstract

Diamond nitrogen-vacancy (NV) color centers have been discovered to introduce new technologies in the field of measurement, especially in the field of quantum measurement, which effectively combines the characteristics of the IT society for rapid iterations in signal collecting and data processing.

In order to directly interact with the operation mechanism behind the experiment, a section of microwave signal source and oscilloscope are programmed and controlled respectively, which contribute to an integrated and automated control system of the Quantum Magnetometer. The framework language used for the program is MATLAB, and the language specification complied with is the SCPI standard command set, while the details of the parameters are set in strict conformity with the physical properties of the instrument. Using the microwave signal source, it completes the remote control of the microwave signal sweep; while on the oscilloscope, the fluorescent signal captured by the oscilloscope is accessed remotely through the program.
